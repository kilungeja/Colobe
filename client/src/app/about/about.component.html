About Colobe

As university students we all need money to live in our environment. Since sometimes we are out of money and we can't easily get people who can trust us to borrow us the money in times of our needs. And sometimes we have a lot of money that we don't know what to do with it to multiply it. From there we discover money is a problem, how to get it in times of emergence and how to benefit in times of excess. The other problem is trust, the presence of rules and regulations will force the borrower to return the money and the lender will be able to give money expecting it will be returned on time, since the interest will be provided.
